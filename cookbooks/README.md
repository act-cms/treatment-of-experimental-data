Lesson Development Cookbooks
============================

Beyond the lesson template, this directory contains a few "cookbooks" for
tools that may make your life easier when developing your lesson materials:

1. `myst.ipynb`
    * Discussion of the enhanced text formatting enabled by using Markedly
    Structured Text (MyST) within JupyterLab
2. `jupyterquiz.ipynb` (coming soon!)
    * Building assessments directly into your lessons
